PACKHACKAPP
ong>​PackHack was born from the lessons of co-managing the life of a family with kids and jobs.  We would fluctuate from over-communication to completly missing the details - and never felt in control of the chaos of family life.  The kid’s project that is due on Monday - forgotten.  Ran out of milk - and missed during a shopping trip.  Whoops, forgot to take out the garbage.  So many details to track and communicate about!

We were exhausted.

And we tried different approaches.  Physical lists get lost and can’t be accessed when needed.  Task apps are too formal, tiresome and turn everything into a chore.  Calendar apps - while important for events - are too much work for simple ad-hoc reminders.

Something was missing. 

So we created a super-fluid and decentralized approach to streamlining family management.  Shopping lists that can be updated even while driving to the store.  Packing lists that the whole family can build together.

Less noise, less work, less nags - and more energy for fun.  Get your family organized, together.
